JR's story.

"Few years ago I decided to buy a Ferrari, at that time I had a BMW Z3 which I used as my everyday car. I decided I had to sell the BMW, partly to finance the Ferrari and buy another car for everyday use. I contacted several dealerships to check which Ferrari models they had for sale, I was mostly looking for a convertible and quickly came across a Ferrari 348 Spider. These were the last models in the 348 series so they had all the latest updates. The car I found was first sold from a dealership in Turin, Italy and somehow made it's way to Japan before being sold many years later to a dealer in Dubai. When I saw the car for the first time it was undergoing a major service but I quickly decided this was the car that I wanted."
